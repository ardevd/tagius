# Privacy Policy for Tagius

*Last updated: 2026-01-16*

## 1. Overview
Tagius ("the App") is an open-source application developed by ardevd @ NorseVault. This App is designed to function as a client for a self-hosted Timetagger instance managed by the user, or the official Timetagger server.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
The App **does not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to the developer or any third parties.

* **No Tracking:** We do not use cookies, analytics, or tracking beacons.
* **No Account Data:** We do not have access to your login credentials or the data stored on your self-hosted server.
* **Server Communication:** All data transmitted by the App is sent directly to the server URL you configure. The developer of this App has no access to this traffic.

## 3. Permissions
The App may request certain permissions (such as Internet access) solely to function as a client for the Timetagger server of your choosing. These permissions are not used to collect data for the developer.

## 4. Third-Party Services
This App does not utilize any third-party services that collect data.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer via [the issue tracker](https://github.com/ardevd/tagius).
